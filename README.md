# Peek Reveal™

> Transforming passive insurance protection into an active, engaging experience

## What is Peek Reveal?

Peek Reveal is an innovative AI-powered platform for the Property & Casualty insurance market that transforms how policyholders interact with their insurance coverage.

### The Core Innovation

Traditional home insurance is passive - you pay premiums and hope you never need it. Peek Reveal makes it **active and valuable** from day one:

1. **Smart Asset Inventory** - AI-powered cataloging of your belongings and property
2. **Intelligent Valuation** - Vision AI estimates replacement costs and reconstruction values
3. **Proactive Risk Prevention** - Identify and address risks before claims happen
4. **Gamified Engagement** - Earn rewards for maintaining your property and reducing risk
5. **Seamless Communication** - Direct channel between policyholders and insurers
6. **Streamlined Claims** - Your inventory becomes instant proof of loss

## The 6-Step Pipeline

```
📸 Smart Inventory → 🏠 Insurance Adhesion → 🛡️ Risk Prevention 
   ↓                     ↓                        ↓
🎮 Gamification  → 💬 Communication → 📋 Claims Settlement
```

### Vision AI at the Core

The platform leverages cutting-edge Vision AI to:
- Analyze photos of rooms, items, and property features
- Estimate building reconstruction costs from visual analysis
- Identify dimensions, materials, and comparable properties
- Detect potential risks and hazards
- Validate claims with existing inventory data
