# Vision & Strategy

## The Problem

Traditional home insurance is:
- **Passive**: No value until you file a claim
- **Disconnected**: Policyholders don't engage with their coverage
- **Inefficient**: Claims require extensive proof of ownership and value
- **Reactive**: Risk prevention happens after incidents, not before

## The Solution: Peek Reveal

Transform insurance from a passive financial product into an active, valuable relationship.

### Core Value Propositions

**For Policyholders:**
- 📱 Easy asset inventory (AI-assisted photo cataloging)
- 💰 Accurate replacement cost estimates
- 🏆 Rewards for risk prevention and property maintenance
- ⚡ Faster, smoother claims with pre-verified inventory
- 🛡️ Proactive risk alerts and prevention tips

**For Insurers:**
- 📊 Better risk assessment data
- 💸 Reduced claims costs through prevention
- 🤝 Improved customer engagement and retention
- ⚖️ Faster claims processing with verified data
- 🎯 Targeted risk mitigation programs

## The Technology

### Vision AI Foundation

The entire platform is built on advanced Vision AI capabilities:

1. **Asset Recognition & Cataloging**
   - Identify items in photos (furniture, electronics, appliances)
   - Estimate age, condition, and replacement value
   - Create searchable inventory database

2. **Building Reconstruction Cost Estimation**
   - Analyze room dimensions from photos
   - Identify materials and finishes
   - Compare with local construction costs
   - Generate accurate replacement cost valuations

3. **Risk Detection**
   - Identify potential hazards (exposed wiring, water damage, etc.)
   - Detect maintenance issues before they become claims
   - Provide actionable prevention recommendations

4. **Claims Validation**
   - Match claimed items against inventory
   - Verify damage with before/after photos
   - Streamline adjuster review process

### Multi-Model Strategy

Use the right AI model for each task:
- **High-volume screening**: Fast, cost-effective models (GPT-5-mini, Gemini 2.0 Flash)
- **Detailed analysis**: Powerful models for complex assessments (GPT-5.4, Claude Opus)
- **Validation**: Cross-check critical decisions with multiple models
- **Cost optimization**: Smart routing based on task complexity

## The 6-Step Pipeline

### 1. Smart Inventory
User photographs their home, room by room. AI catalogs items, estimates values, identifies materials and features.

### 2. Insurance Adhesion
Inventory data feeds into policy underwriting and valuation. More accurate coverage, better premiums for well-maintained properties.

### 3. Risk Prevention
AI identifies potential risks. Platform sends alerts and prevention tips. Users can request professional inspections.

### 4. Gamification
Earn points for:
- Completing inventory updates
- Acting on risk prevention recommendations
- Maintaining property (annual check-ins)
- Referring friends

Rewards: Premium discounts, coverage upgrades, partner offers.

### 5. Communication Channel
Direct line between policyholder and insurer:
- Risk alerts and prevention tips
- Policy updates and renewals
- Claims support
- Maintenance reminders

### 6. Claims Settlement
When a claim occurs:
- Existing inventory = instant proof of ownership
- Before photos validate condition and value
- AI assists with damage assessment
- Faster, more accurate settlements
